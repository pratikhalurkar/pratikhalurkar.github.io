Project Management Portfolio

Below are the project management files that I have done during the capstone project of Sauce & Spoon in Google Project management professional certificate.

My Google Project Management course capstone was Sauce & Spoon. Throughout the course of the project, I was responsible for: Documents and other resources were analyzed to determine project needs, evaluate stakeholders, and identify and solve problems. Finished a project charter and used it to get everyone on the same page regarding the project's objectives. refined the SMART objectives of the project and used stakeholder negotiation skills to set priorities. I reviewed existing project documentation, conducted online research, and analyzed key conversations to identify project tasks and milestones, which I then documented and prioritized in a project plan. Proven ability to effectively communicate in order to generate reliable project timeline estimates. To guarantee the project met the required quality, we defined and outlined a set of quality management standards and assessed its progress against those criteria. Created evaluation questions based on survey responses and understood the importance of sharing qualitative data. I picked up some tricks for leading a fruitful retrospective by getting people involved, making them take responsibility, and keeping things upbeat. I have improved my reporting skills and learned to effectively convey project issues to stakeholders.
